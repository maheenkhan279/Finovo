How does the gamification work?

Our platform uses interactive games and challenges to teach financial concepts. Start with beginner games to learn the basics, then progress to more advanced simulations.

Yes, creating a free account is required to access our financial games. Registration is quick and easy.

FINOVO covers needs vs wants, budgeting, saving, investing, stock market basics, and personal finance management organized into beginner, moderate, and advanced levels.
